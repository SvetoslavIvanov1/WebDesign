Available Holidays

Majorca boasts beautiful beaches, exciting nightlife and restaurants on tap, making it a great choice for groups and families alike. Families are well catered for, with plenty of resorts featuring kids clubs and activities for teens, parents will be able to sit back and relax. There are excursions if you want to explore the island too. With more than 1,000 hotels and apartments to choose from, whether you’re looking for 5-star luxury or are on a budget, you can plan a perfect holiday..
From the Romans to the Renaissance, Rome has played a starring role in many mega moments in time. It's also the reason behind its extensive fine collection of ancient sites and artistic masterpieces. Long known as the 'Eternal City', Rome is certain to leave a lasting impression on you.
Sunny Beach on the southern Black Sea coast is the biggest and one of the most popular beach resorts in Bulgaria with an astonishing 8km stretch of golden sand. Sunny Beach is situated only 30km from Bourgas airport which is ideal for families, couples and groups wanting a short transfer. Popular attractions and activities include water sports, taking in historical sights and relaxing by the beach. The resort is also close to two waterparks as well the historical site of Nessebar. Families are well catered for with an abundance of beach hotels with kids clubs and activities such as mini golf, playgrounds and beach activities such as volleyball, jet skiing and paragliding.
Mykonos holidays. Mykonos is one of the most vibrant holiday destinations in Europe and has earned an enviable reputation for its nightlife. The island is particularly famous for its beautiful whitewashed buildings, which complement the surrounding turquoise sea and the island's unspoilt sandy beaches.
